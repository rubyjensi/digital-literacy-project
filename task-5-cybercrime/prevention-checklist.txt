Stay Safe Online: Cyber Security Checklist for College Students 

Here are 8 actionable tips to protect your digital and financial identity: 

1. Keep Social Media Private: Restrict your personal profiles to "Friends Only" to prevent strangers from scraping your photos.
2. Verify Before Trusting: Never trust unsolicited job offers or messages on WhatsApp/Telegram that promise quick money.
3. Use Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Enable 2FA on all important accounts, especially your college email.
4. Avoid Public Wi-Fi for Banking: Never make fee payments or check your bank balance while connected to free public Wi-Fi.
5. Think Before Clicking: Do not click on random links claiming you have won a prize.
6. Update Your Software: Regularly update your phone's OS and apps.
7. FINANCIAL SAFETY 1 (The Golden Rule of UPI): You ONLY need to enter your UPI PIN when you are SENDING money. You NEVER need a PIN to receive money. 
8. FINANCIAL SAFETY 2 (Scan with Caution): Never scan a random QR code sent by an unknown person to "claim a refund".
