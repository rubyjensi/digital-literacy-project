# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

**5 DO's (What you should do):**
1. **Do maintain a professional presence:** Always keep your LinkedIn profile updated with your latest education and skills.
2. **Do review privacy settings:** Regularly check who can see your posts and personal information on platforms like Instagram and Facebook.
3. **Do think before you post:** Remember that the internet is permanent. Don't post anything you wouldn't want a future employer to see.
4. **Do verify information:** Always fact-check news or forwards before sharing them on your stories or family WhatsApp groups.
5. **Do network actively:** Use social media to connect with college alumni, professors, and industry professionals in your field.

**5 DON'Ts (What you should avoid):**
1. **Don't overshare personal details:** Avoid posting your exact live location, phone number, or hostel room details publicly.
2. **Don't engage in cyberbullying:** Never participate in online trolling, hate speech, or making derogatory comments about peers.
3. **Don't click on unverified links:** Avoid clicking on suspicious links in DMs promising free giveaways or followers.
4. **Don't use college networks for inappropriate browsing:** Always follow the university's IT policy when using campus Wi-Fi.
5. **Don't complain about professors or peers online:** Venting frustrations about your university or specific individuals on public platforms can severely harm your reputation.
